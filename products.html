Jordan 1 Chicago Lost & Found

Description: Air Jordan 1 Retros with a Varsity Red/Black-Sail-Muslin colorway. 2022 is the year that the colorway returns with an added vintage look. Pre-yellowed accents and cracked leather uppers showcase a fabricated look of age and wear.

Jordan 2 Retro OG Chicago

Description: Air Jordan 2 classic red, white, and black scheme was of course based on the Chicago Bulls. white reptile-embossed leather uppers, Varsity Red reptile spine design on the heel, and Varsity Red signature Wings logo on the tongue.

Jordan 3 Retro SE Unite (Chicago Exclusive)

Description: Air Jordan 3 with a colorway of Varsity Red/VARSITY Red-Cement Grey this Jordan 3 is composed of a fire red tumbled leather upper with signature Elephant print overlays and a black rubber heel tab displaying "Nike Chi" branding. Gray leather on the perforated eyelets, ankle collar, and smooth leather tongue with a red Jumpman logo completes the design.

Jordan 4 Retro Fire Red (2020)

Description: Air Jordan 4 with a colorway of White/Black-Tech Grey-Fire Red. This Jordan 4 consists of a white leather upper with black and Fire Red detailing. Rubber netted underlays, black heel tabs with "Nike Air" branding, and black with fire red midsoles completes this design.

Jordan 5 Retro Fire Red Silver Tongue (2020)

Description: Air Jordan 5 with a colorway of White/Black-Metallic Silver-Fire Red This Jordan 5 consists of a white leather upper with netted underlays and Fire Red detailing. A silver 3M reflective tongue with a red Jumpman logo, black and Fire Red midsole, and an icy translucent outsole completes the design.
