OG's Only

Founded Dec 4, 2023.

Aaman and his colleagues came together and started OG’s Only, a new shoe company tasked with the objective of distributing Jordan Retro’s to the modern day consumer. They entered the footwear industry to keep retro sneakers in circulation, and to allow modern buyers to enjoy Retro Jordans. Another objective of theirs was the preservation of historical footwear that had an astronomical impact on the industry and everyone who wore them. Shoe companies such as Nike and Adidas have dominated the market and brought so many unforgettable collaborations and original creations to the world, but unfortunately many of those works of arts are lost to time as companies stop profiting and stop producing their shoes.
